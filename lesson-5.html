About Wendi

I was born in California, however, after turning 7 years old, my parents and I moved to Bozeman, Montana. I grew up with two very strict parents that encouraged me to excel in school and participate in many after school activiteis.

Due to this, I become involved in volleball, basketball, track and skiing. I grew up with a lot of school spirit and drive. Summers in Montana were my favorite. My mom was a teacher and my dad retired early, which left summers wide open. I'll never forget my great memories of flyfishing and camping all summer long.

The way I was raised has definetly encouraged me to stay active and outdoorsey now into my adult life. I spend much of my time outdoors camping, hiking, exploring, etc with my chocolate lab, Coby. After earning my bachelor's degree from Arizona State University, I was tired of the desert and needed a change. One of the many reason that brought my to Washington was the beautiful weather year-round.

Although I have lived in five different states and many cities, I am happy to call Seattle home.
